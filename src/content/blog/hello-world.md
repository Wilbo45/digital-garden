Welcome to my digital garden.

This is a place for long-form writing, interactive web experiments, and information graphics. Unlike a traditional blog, a digital garden is a place to tend ideas over time — planting seeds, letting them grow, occasionally pruning.

## What you'll find here

- **Writing** — essays and notes on design, technology, and data
- **Interactive apps** — small, focused web experiments
- **Information graphics** — visual explorations of interesting datasets

## Why "digital garden"?

The metaphor resonates because the best ideas aren't published once and forgotten — they're tended. Revisited. Improved. This site is built to support that kind of iterative thinking.

More to come.
